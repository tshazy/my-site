About

Grew up in LA, then migrated to Northern California in 2005 to attend Stanford. Got a degree in Product Design and now can't stop thinking about products...

Founded MindSumo in 2011 to help companies solve problems by crowdsourcing new ideas from students and recent grads. Grew it to 350,000+ users and 100 Fortune 500 clients.

My wife and I started a full-time MBA back at Stanford in 2017 before moving to Seattle, where I work on the Microsoft Teams product.
